Always

Sempre

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I always brush my teeth.

    Eu sempre escovo minha dentes.

  • The sun always rises in the morning.

    O sol nasce sempre de manhã.

  • My mom always cooks dinner.

    A minha mãe sempre cozinha o jantar.

  • We always go to the park on Sundays.

    Nós sempre vamos ao parque aos domingos.

  • We always use fire to cook food.

    Nós sempre usamos fogo para cozinhar comida.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Driving License

Carta de conducao

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • A driving license allows you to drive a vehicle.

    Uma carta de condução permite-lhe conduzir um veículo.

  • You need to pass a test to get a driving license.

    É preciso passar num exame para obter a carta de condução.

  • A driving license has your picture on it.

    Uma carta de condução tem a sua fotografia

  • It is illegal to drive without a license.

    É ilegal conduzir sem carta.

  • You must renew your driving license every few years.

    Tem de renovar a sua carta de condução de alguns em alguns anos.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !
