Lesson 10

Explore a variety of resources to master the beautiful language of European Portuguese!

Love

Amor

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I love to travel the world.

    Eu amo viajar pelo mundo. ( pelo mundo: around the world )

                          Eu: This is the subject pronoun in 
                      Portuguese, meaning "I."

                      amo: This is the present tense of 
                      the verb "amar," which means "to love."

                      viajar: This is the infinitive verb 
                      "to travel."

                      pelo: This is the preposition "por" 
                      (by, through) contracted with the 
                      definite article "o" (masculine singular), 
                      meaning "through the."

                      mundo: This is the noun "world" (masculine).

  • Love is a powerful emotion.

    O amor é uma emoção poderosa. ( poderosa: powerful )

                            O: The (masculine singular definite article)
                        amor: love (masculine noun)
                        é: is (present tense of the verb "ser")
                        uma: a (feminine singular indefinite article)
                        emoção: emotion (feminine noun)
                        poderosa: powerful (feminine adjective)

  • She loves to cook for the family.

    Ela ama cozinhar para a família.

  • True love is rare.

    O amor verdadeiro é raro.

                            O: The (masculine singular definite article)
                        verdadeiro: true
                        amor: love (masculine noun)
                        é: is (present tense of the verb "ser")
                        raro: rare

  • He loves classical music.

    Ele ama música clássica. ( ama: loves (present tense of "amar") )

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Happy

Feliz

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I am very happy.

    Estou muito feliz.

  • They are happy.

    Eles estão felizes.

  • The child is very happy.

    A criança está muito feliz.

  • I was very happy with the news.

    Fiquei muito feliz com a notícia. ( Fiquei: I was (past tense of "ficar") )

                            Eu: This is the subject pronoun in 
                        Portuguese, meaning "I."
                        
                        fiquei: This is the past tense of 
                        the verb "ficar," which means 
                        "to become" or "to be." Here, it 
                        expresses the state of being happy.

                        muito: This adverb intensifies the 
                        adjective "feliz," meaning "very."

                        feliz: This is the adjective "happy."

                        com: This is the preposition "with."

                        a: This is the definite article for 
                        feminine singular nouns, meaning "the."

                        notícia: This is the noun "news" (feminine).

  • I hope you are happy.

    Espero que você esteja feliz.

                            Espero: This is the present tense of the verb "esperar" 
                        (to hope, to expect, to wait).

                        "Esperar" has a broader meaning than just "to wait." 
                        It encompasses the idea of anticipating, expecting, 
                        or desiring something.

                        In this context, "Espero" conveys the speaker's 
                        desire for the other person's happiness.

                        que: This is a conjunction meaning "that."

                        você: This is the pronoun "you" (informal).

                        seja: This is the present subjunctive of 
                        the verb "ser" (to be), used here to 
                        express a hope or wish.

                        feliz: This is the adjective "happy."

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Sad

Triste

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I am sad.

    Estou triste.

  • He is sad.

    Ele está triste.

  • The child is sad.

    A criança está triste.

  • I was sad with the news.

    Fiquei triste com a notícia. ( Fiquei: I was (past tense of "ficar") )

                          Eu: This is the subject pronoun 
                      in Portuguese, meaning "I."

                      fiquei: This is the past tense 
                      of the verb "ficar," which means 
                      "to become" or "to be." Here, 
                      it expresses the state of being sad.

                      triste: This is the adjective "sad."

                      com: This is the preposition "with."

                      a: This is the definite article for 
                      feminine singular nouns, meaning "the."

                      notícia: This is the noun "news" (feminine).

  • I hope you are not sad.

    Espero que você não esteja triste. ( Espero: I hope (present tense of "esperar") && esteja: are (present tense of "estar"))

                          Espero: This is the present tense 
                      of the verb "esperar" (to hope).

                      que: This is a conjunction meaning "that."

                      você: This is the pronoun "you" (informal).

                      não: This is the negation "not."

                      esteja: This is the present subjunctive 
                      of the verb "estar" (to be), used here 
                      to express a hope or wish.

                      triste: This is the adjective "sad."

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Rich

Rico

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • He is rich.

    Ele é rico.

  • She is very rich.

    Ela é muito rica.

  • Her family is very rich.

    A família dela é muito rica.

                          A: The (feminine singular definite article)
                      família: family (feminine noun)
                      dela: of her
                      é: is (present tense of the verb "ser")
                      muito: very
                      rica: rich (feminine adjective)

  • He became rich after winning the lottery.

    Ficou rico depois de ganhar na loteria.

                          Ele: He (masculine pronoun)
                      ficou: became (past tense of "ficar")
                      rico: rich (masculine adjective)
                      depois de: after
                      ganhar: winning (infinitive of "ganhar")
                      na: in the
                      loteria: lottery (feminine noun)

  • Being rich does not guarantee happiness.

    Ser rico não garante a felicidade. ( a felicidade: happiness (feminine) ) && ( garante: guarantees (present tense of "garantir") )

                          Ser: To be (infinitive verb)
                      rico: rich (masculine adjective)
                      não: not
                      garante: guarantees (present tense of the verb "garantir")
                      felicidade: happiness (feminine noun)

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

In(Inside)

Dentro

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • The book is on the table

    O livro está sobre a mesa

                        O: The (masculine singular definite article)
                    livro: book (masculine noun)
                    está: is (present tense of the verb "estar" - to be)
                    sobre: on (preposition)
                    a: the (feminine singular definite article)
                    mesa: table (feminine noun)

  • I live in Lisbon.

    Moro em Lisboa.

  • I will go out in five minutes.

    Vou sair em cinco minutos.

  • I put the money in the wallet.

    Coloquei o dinheiro na carteira. ( Coloquei: I put (past tense of "colocar") )

                          Eu: I (subject pronoun)
                      coloquei: I put (past tense of "colocar")
                      o: the (masculine singular definite article)
                      dinheiro: money (masculine noun)
                      na: in the (feminine singular)
                      carteira: wallet (feminine noun)

  • I saw a cat in the garden.

    Vi um gato no jardim.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

Out

Fora

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • He/She went out of the house.

    Saiu de casa. ( Saiu: He/She went out (past tense of "sair") )

  • I am going to go out for dinner.

    Vou sair para jantar.

  • He/She left the garbage outside.

    Deixou o lixo lá fora.

                          Ela: She (feminine pronoun)
                      deixou: left (past tense of "deixar")
                      o: the (masculine singular definite article)
                      lixo: garbage (masculine noun)
                      lá fora: outside

  • The dog is playing outside.

    O cão está brincando lá fora.

  • I stayed outside.

    Fiquei do lado de fora.

                          Eu: I (subject pronoun)
                      
                      fiquei: stayed (past tense of "ficar") 
                      - Here, "ficar" means "to remain" or "to stay."

                      do lado de fora: outside

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !
