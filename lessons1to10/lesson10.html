Lesson 10

Explore a variety of resources to master the beautiful language of European Portuguese!

Love

Amor

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I love to travel the world.

    Eu amo viajar pelo mundo. ( pelo mundo: around the world )

  • Love is a powerful emotion.

    O amor é uma emoção poderosa. ( poderosa: powerful )

  • She loves to cook for the family.

    Ela ama cozinhar para a família.

  • True love is rare.

    O amor verdadeiro é raro.

  • He loves classical music.

    Ele ama música clássica. ( ama: loves (present tense of "amar") )

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Rich

Rico

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • He is rich.

    Ele é rico.

  • She is very rich.

    Ela é muito rica.

  • Her family is very rich.

    A família dela é muito rica.

  • He became rich after winning the lottery.

    Ficou rico depois de ganhar na loteria.

  • Being rich does not guarantee happiness.

    Ser rico não garante a felicidade. ( a felicidade: happiness (feminine) ) && ( garante: guarantees (present tense of "garantir") )

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

Out

Fora

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • He/She went out of the house.

    Saiu de casa. ( Saiu: He/She went out (past tense of "sair") )

  • I am going to go out for dinner.

    Vou sair para jantar.

  • He/She left the garbage outside.

    Deixou o lixo lá fora.

  • The dog is playing outside.

    O cão está brincando lá fora.

  • I stayed outside.

    Fiquei do lado de fora.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !
