Sir

Senhor

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • Good morning, Sir.

    Bom dia, Senhor.

  • Thank you, Sir.

    Obrigado, Senhor.

  • Excuse me, Sir.

    Com licença, Senhor.

  • Can you help me, Sir?

    O Senhor pode me ajudar?

  • Good afternoon, Sir.

    Boa tarde, Senhor.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Friend

Amigo or Amiga

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I have many friends.

    Tenho muitos amigos.

                          Tenho: I have (present tense of "ter")
                      muitos: many (masculine plural)
                      amigos: friends (masculine plural)

  • I called my friend yesterday.

    Liguei para o meu amigo ontem.

                            Liguei: I called (past tense of "ligar")
                        para: for
                        o meu: my (masculine singular)
                        amigo: friend (masculine singular)
                        ontem: yesterday

  • She is my best friend.

    Ela é minha melhor amiga.

                            Ela: She
                        é: is (present tense of "ser")
                        minha: my (feminine singular)
                        melhor: best
                        amiga: friend (feminine singular)

  • I met my friend at the cafe.

    Encontrei meu amigo no café.

                            Encontrei: I met (past tense of "encontrar")
                        meu: my (masculine singular)
                        amigo: friend (masculine singular)
                        no: at the (masculine singular)
                        café: cafe

  • My friends and I went to the movies.

    Meus amigos e eu fomos ao cinema.

                            Meus amigos: My friends (masculine plural)
                        e: and
                        eu: I
                        fomos: we went (past tense of "ir")
                        ao: to the (masculine singular)
                        cinema: cinema

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Man

Homen

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • The man is sitting.

    O homem está sentado.

  • The man works in the office.

    O homem trabalha no escritório.

  • I saw a man on the street.

    Vi um homem na rua.

                          Eu: This is the subject pronoun in Portuguese,
                      meaning "I."

                      vi: This is the past tense of the verb "ver," 
                      which means "to see."

                      um: This is the indefinite article for 
                      masculine singular nouns, meaning "a" or "one."

                      homem: This is the noun "man" (masculine).

                      na: This is the preposition "em" (in) 
                      contracted with the definite article 
                      "a" (feminine singular), meaning "in the."
                      
                      rua: This is the noun "street" (feminine).

  • The man is tall.

    O homem é alto.

  • The man smiled at me.

    O homem sorriu para mim.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !
