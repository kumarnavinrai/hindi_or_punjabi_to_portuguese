Lesson 8

Explore a variety of resources to master the beautiful language of European Portuguese!

Front

Frente

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • The front door is open.

    A porta da frente está aberta.

  • There is a tree in front of the house.

    Há uma árvore na frente da casa.

  • I sat in the front of the bus.

    Sentei-me na frente do autocarro.

  • The garden is in front of the house.

    O jardim fica na frente da casa.

  • He was sitting in front of me.

    Ele estava sentado na frente de mim.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Back

Trás

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I went back.

    Voltei para trás.

  • I left the backpack on my back.

    Deixei a mochila às minhas costas.

  • He went back.

    Ele voltou para trás.

  • Take a step back.

    Dê um passo atrás.

  • Back pain is very common.

    A dor nas costas é muito comum.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

Up

Cima

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • Look up!

    Olhe para cima!

  • He went up the stairs.

    Subiu as escadas.

  • The plane is going up.

    O avião está a subir. (está a subir: is going up (gerund))

  • Stand up from the chair.

    Levante-se da cadeira. ( Stand up (imperative form of "levantar-se" - reflexive verb) )

  • The price of bread went up.

    O preço do pão subiu.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Down

Baixo

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • He went down the stairs.

    Desceu as escadas. ( Desceu: He went down (past tense of "descer") )

  • Look down.

    Olhe para baixo.

  • The plane is going down.

    O avião está a descer. ( está a descer: is going down (gerund) )

  • Sit down.

    Sente-se: Sit down (imperative form of "sentar-se" - reflexive verb)

  • The price of sugar went down.

    O preço do açúcar baixou.

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !
