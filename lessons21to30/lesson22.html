Laugh

Rir

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I laugh every day.

    Eu rio todos os dias.

                          Breakdown:

                      Eu: I
                      rio: I laugh (present tense of "rir")
                      todos: all
                      os: the
                      dias: days

  • He laughed at the joke.

    Ele riu da piada.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        riu: laughed (past tense of "rir")
                        da: of the
                        piada: joke

  • The children laughed loudly.

    As crianças riram alto.

                            Breakdown:

                        As: The
                        crianças: children
                        riram: laughed (past tense of "rir")
                        alto: loudly

  • I always laugh at his jokes.

    É difícil acreditar que ele se foi.

                            Breakdown:

                        Sempre: Always
                        rio: I laugh (present tense of "rir")
                        das: of the
                        suas: his
                        piadas: jokes

  • She has a contagious laugh.

    Ela tem uma risada contagiante.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ela: She
                        tem: has
                        uma: a (feminine singular)
                        risada: laugh
                        contagiante: contagious

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Cry

Chorar

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I cry when I'm sad.

    Eu choro quando estou triste.

                          Breakdown:

                      Eu: I
                      choro: I cry (present tense of "chorar")
                      quando: when
                      estou: I am
                      triste: sad

  • The baby cried all night.

    O bebê chorou a noite toda.

                            Breakdown:

                        O: The
                        bebê: baby
                        chorou: cried (past tense of "chorar")
                        a: the
                        noite: night
                        toda: whole

  • I started to cry when I heard the news.

    Comecei a chorar quando ouvi a notícia.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I (implied in "Comecei")
                        Comecei: I started (past tense of "começar")
                        a: to
                        chorar: cry
                        quando: when
                        ouvi: I heard (past tense of "ouvir")
                        a: the
                        notícia: news

  • The movie made me cry.

    O filme me fez chorar.

                            Breakdown:

                        O: The
                        filme: movie
                        me: me
                        fez: made (past tense of "fazer")
                        chorar: cry

  • She cried for hours.

    Ela chorou por horas.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ela: She
                        chorou: cried (past tense of "chorar")
                        por: for
                        horas: hours

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !
