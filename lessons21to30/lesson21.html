Right

Certo

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • You are right.

    Você está certo/certa.

                          Breakdown:

                      Você: You
                      está: are
                      certo/certa: right (masculine/feminine)

  • Turn right at the next corner.

    Vire à direita na próxima esquina.

                            Breakdown:

                        Vire: Turn (imperative of "virar")
                        à: to the (feminine singular)
                        direita: right
                        na: at the
                        próxima: next
                        esquina: corner

  • That's the right answer.

    Essa é a resposta certa.

                            Breakdown:

                        Essa: That (feminine singular)
                        é: is
                        a: the
                        resposta: answer
                        certa: right (feminine)

  • I'm sure you're right.

    Tenho certeza que você está certo/certa.

                            Breakdown:

                        Tenho: I have
                        certeza: certainty
                        que: that
                        você: you
                        está: are
                        certo/certa: right (masculine/feminine)

  • You have the right to remain silent.

    Você tem o direito de permanecer calado/calada.

                            Breakdown:

                        Você: You
                        tem: have
                        o: the
                        direito: right
                        de: of
                        permanecer: to remain
                        calado/calada: silent (masculine/feminine)

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

                    prédio: building
                    velho: old
                    é: is
                    feio: ugly

  • That's an ugly dress.

    Esse é um vestido feio.

                          Breakdown:

                      Esse: That (masculine singular)
                      é: is
                      um: a (masculine singular)

                      feio: ugly

  • The weather is ugly today.

    O tempo está feio hoje.

                          Breakdown:

Clean

Limpo

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • The house is very clean.

    A casa está muito limpa.

                        Breakdown:

                    A: The
                    casa: house
                    está: is
                    muito: very
                    limpa: clean (feminine)

  • I need to clean the kitchen.

    Preciso limpar a cozinha.

                          Breakdown:

                      Preciso: I need
                      limpar: to clean
                      a: the
                      cozinha: kitchen

  • I always wear clean clothes.

    Sempre uso roupas limpas.

                          Breakdown:

                      Sempre: Always
                      uso: I use/wear
                      roupas: clothes
                      limpas: clean (feminine plural)

  • The water is clean.

    A água está limpa.

                          Breakdown:

                      A: The
                      água: water
                      está: is
                      limpa: clean (feminine)

  • I cleaned my room yesterday.

    Limpei o meu quarto ontem.

                          Breakdown:

                      Eu: I (implied in "Limpei")
                      Limpei: I cleaned (past tense of "limpar")
                      o: the
                      meu: my
                      quarto: room
                      ontem: yesterday

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.
