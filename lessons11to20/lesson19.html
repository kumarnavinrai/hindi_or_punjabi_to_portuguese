Hot

Quente

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • The weather is hot today.

    O tempo está quente hoje.

                          Breakdown:

                      O: The
                      tempo: weather
                      está: is
                      quente: hot

  • I feel hot.

    Estou com calor.

                            Breakdown:

                        Estou: I am
                        com: with
                        calor: heat

  • This soup is too hot to eat.

    Esta sopa está muito quente para comer.

                            Breakdown:

                        Esta: This
                        sopa: soup
                        está: is
                        muito: very
                        quente: hot
                        para: to
                        comer: ea

  • The chili peppers are very hot.

    As pimentas são muito picantes.

                            Breakdown:

                        As: The
                        pimentas: chili peppers
                        são: are
                        muito: very
                        picantes: hot (spicy)

  • I need a cold drink; I'm feeling hot.

    Preciso de uma bebida fria; estou com calor.

                            Breakdown:

                        Preciso: I need
                        de: of
                        uma: a (feminine singular)
                        bebida: drink
                        fria: cold
                        estou: I am
                        com: with
                        calor: heat

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

  • The weather is cold today.

    O tempo está frio hoje.

                          Breakdown:

                      O: The
                      tempo: weather

                      frio: cold

  • I'm feeling cold.

    Estou com frio.

                            Breakdown:

                        Estou: I am
                        com: with
                        frio: cold

  • The water in the pool is cold.

    A água da piscina está fria.

                            Breakdown:

                        A: The
                        água: water
                        da: of the
                        piscina: pool
                        está: is
                        fria: cold

  • I need to wear a coat; it's cold outside.

    Preciso vestir um casaco; está frio lá fora.

                            Breakdown:

                        Preciso: I need

                        casaco: coat
                        está: is
                        frio: cold
                        lá: there
                        fora: outside

  • I ate some cold pizza for lunch.

    Comi uma pizza fria no almoço.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I (implied in "Comi")
                        Comi: I ate (past tense of "comer")
                        uma: a (feminine singular)
                        pizza: pizza
                        fria: cold
                        no: at the
                        almoço: lunch

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.
