Brain

Cérebro

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I use my brain to think.

    Eu uso o meu cérebro para pensar.

                          Breakdown:

                      Eu: I
                      uso: use (present tense of "usar")
                      o: the
                      meu: my
                      cérebro: brain
                      para: to
                      pensar: think

  • He is very intelligent; he has a big brain.

    Ele é muito inteligente; tem um cérebro grande.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        é: is
                        muito: very
                        inteligente: intelligent
                        tem: has
                        um: a (masculine singular)
                        cérebro: brain
                        grande: big

  • Exercise is good for the brain.

    Exercício é bom para o cérebro.

                            Breakdown:

                        Exercício: Exercise
                        é: is
                        bom: good
                        para: for
                        o: the
                        cérebro: brain

  • I need to use my brain more.

    Preciso usar mais o meu cérebro.

                            Breakdown:

                        Preciso: I need
                        usar: to use
                        mais: more
                        o: the
                        meu: my
                        cérebro: brain

  • He is a brain surgeon.

    Ele é um neurocirurgião.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        é: is
                        um: a (masculine singular)
                        neurocirurgião: neurosurgeon

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

                            Breakdown:

    Eu escuto meu coração.

                            Breakdown:
