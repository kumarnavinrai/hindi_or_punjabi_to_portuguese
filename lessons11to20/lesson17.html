Work

Trabalho

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I go to work every day.

    Eu vou trabalhar todos os dias.

                          Breakdown:

                      Eu: I
                      vou: go (present tense of "ir")
                      trabalhar: to work
                      todos: all
                      os: the
                      dias: days

  • I love my work.

    Eu amo o meu trabalho.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I
                        amo: love
                        o: the
                        meu: my
                        trabalho: work

  • He is at work now.

    Ele está no trabalho agora.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        está: is
                        no: at the
                        trabalho: work
                        agora: now

  • I have a lot of work to do.

    Tenho muito trabalho para fazer.

                            Breakdown:

                        Tenho: I have
                        muito: a lot of
                        trabalho: work
                        para: to
                        fazer: do

  • I'm looking for a new job.

    Estou procurando um novo emprego.

                            Breakdown:

                        Estou: I am
                        procurando: looking for
                        um: a (masculine singular)
                        novo: new
                        emprego: job

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Holiday

Feriado

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • The holiday season is my favorite time of year.

    A época das festas é a minha época favorita do ano.

                          Breakdown:

                      A: The
                      época: season
                      das: of the
                      festas: holidays
                      é: is
                      a: the
                      minha: my
                      época: time
                      favorita: favorite
                      do: of the
                      ano: year

  • Good morning/Good afternoon! I would like to know if there are any job openings available here.

    Bom dia/Boa tarde! Gostaria de saber se há vagas de emprego disponíveis aqui.

                            Breakdown:

                        Bom dia/Boa tarde!

                        Bom dia: Good morning.
                        Boa tarde: Good afternoon.
                        These are polite greetings used when entering a shop or store.

                        Gostaria de saber: I would like to know.

                        This phrase expresses your desire to obtain information.
                        se há: if there are

                        This conditional clause introduces the question about job openings.

                        vagas de emprego: job openings

                        vagas: vacancies/openings
                        de emprego: of employment

                        disponíveis: available

                        This adjective describes the status of the job openings.

                        aqui: here

  • We celebrated the holiday with a big feast.

    Celebramos o feriado com uma grande festa.

                            Breakdown:

                        Celebramos: We celebrated (past tense of "celebrar")
                        o: the
                        feriado: holiday
                        com: with
                        uma: a (feminine singular)
                        grande: big
                        festa: feast

  • The holiday decorations were beautiful.

    As decorações de Natal eram lindas.

                            Breakdown:

                        As: The
                        decorações: decorations
                        de: of
                        Natal: Christmas
                        eram: were
                        lindas: beautiful

  • I took a holiday from work last week.

    Tirei férias do trabalho na semana passada.

                            Breakdown:

                        Tirei: I took (past tense of "tirar")
                        férias: holiday (plural)
                        do: from
                        trabalho: work
                        na: in the
                        semana: week
                        passada: past

  • This is a national holiday.

    Este é um feriado nacional.

                            Breakdown:

                        Este: This
                        é: is
                        um: a (masculine singular)
                        feriado: holiday
                        nacional: national

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

                    o: the
                    recibo: receipt
                    para: for

                    devolução: return

  • Can I have a receipt for this purchase?
