Lesson 17

Explore a variety of resources to master the beautiful language of European Portuguese!

Work

Trabalho

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • Bye for now!

    Até logo!

                          Breakdown:

                      Breakdown:
                      Até: Until
                      logo: soon

  • Goodbye, my friend!

    Adeus, meu amigo!

                            Breakdown:

                        Adeus: Goodbye
                        meu: my
                        amigo: friend

  • Bye, I'll see you tomorrow.

    Tchau, te vejo amanhã.

                            Breakdown:

                        Tchau: Bye
                        te: you (informal)
                        vejo: see
                        amanhã: tomorrow

  • Bye, have a nice day!

    Tchau, tenha um bom dia!

                            Breakdown:

                        Tchau: Bye
                        tenha: have
                        um: a (masculine singular)
                        bom: good
                        dia: day

  • Bye, take care!

    Tchau, cuide-se!

                            Breakdown:

                        Tchau: Bye
                        cuide-se: take care (reflexive imperative)

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !
