Head

Cabeça

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I have a headache.

    Tenho dor de cabeça.

                          Breakdown:

                      Tenho: I have (present tense of "ter")
                      dor: pain
                      de: of
                      cabeça: head

  • He shook his head.

    Ele sacudiu a cabeça.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        sacudiu: shook (past tense of "sacudir")
                        a: the (feminine)
                        cabeça: head

  • She scratched her head.

    Ela coçou a cabeça.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ela: She
                        coçou: scratched (past tense of "coçar")
                        a: the (feminine)
                        cabeça: head

  • The ball hit his head.

    A bola bateu na cabeça dele.

                            Breakdown:

                        A bola: The ball (feminine)
                        bateu: hit (past tense of "bater")
                        na: on the (feminine)
                        cabeça: head
                        dele: of him

  • I wear a hat on my head.

    Eu uso um chapéu na cabeça.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I
                        uso: I use/wear (present tense of "usar")
                        um: a (masculine)
                        chapéu: hat
                        na: on the (feminine)
                        cabeça: head

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !
