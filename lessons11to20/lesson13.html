Arm

Braço

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I raised my arm.

    Levantei o braço.

                          Breakdown:

                      Levantei: I raised (past tense of "levantar")
                      o braço: the arm (masculine)

  • She broke her arm.

    Ela quebrou o braço.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ela: She
                        quebrou: broke (past tense of "quebrar")
                        o braço: the arm (masculine)

  • He held the baby in his arms.

    Ele segurou o bebê nos braços.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        segurou: held (past tense of "segurar")
                        o bebê: the baby (masculine)
                        nos braços: in the arms (masculine plural)

  • She crossed her arms.

    Ela cruzou os braços.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ela: She
                        cruzou: crossed (past tense of "cruzar")
                        os braços: the arms (masculine plural)

  • I have a sore arm.

    Tenho o braço dolorido.

                            Breakdown:

                        Tenho: I have (present tense of "ter")
                        o braço: the arm (masculine)
                        dolorido: sore

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Pulse

Pulso

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I checked my pulse.

    Verifiquei o pulso.

                        Breakdown:

                    Verifiquei: I checked (past tense of "verificar")
                    o pulso: the pulse (masculine)

  • The doctor felt for my pulse.

    O médico apalpou o meu pulso. / O médico sentiu-me o pulso.

                          Breakdown:

                      O médico: The doctor (masculine)
                      apalpou: felt (past tense of "apalpar")
                      o meu: my (masculine)
                      pulso: pulse


                      O médico: The doctor (masculine)
                      sentiu-me: sense or feel (past tense of "sentir")
                      o meu: my (masculine)
                      pulso: pulse

  • My pulse is racing.

    O meu pulso está acelerado.

                          Breakdown:

                      O meu: My (masculine)
                      pulso: pulse
                      está: is (present tense of "estar")
                      acelerado: racing, accelerated

  • He tried to calm his racing pulse.

    Ele tentou acalmar o pulso acelerado.

                          Breakdown:

                      Ele: He
                      tentou: tried (past tense of "tentar")
                      acalmar: to calm
                      o pulso: the pulse (masculine)
                      acelerado: racing, accelerated

  • A healthy pulse is essential for good health.

    Um pulso saudável é essencial para uma boa saúde.

                          Breakdown:

                      Um: A (masculine)
                      pulso: pulse
                      saudável: healthy
                      é: is (present tense of "ser")
                      essencial: essential
                      para: for
                      uma: a (feminine)
                      boa: good
                      saúde: health

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !
