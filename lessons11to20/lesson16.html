Bye

Tchau

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • Bye for now!

    Até logo!

                          Breakdown:

                      Breakdown:
                      Até: Until
                      logo: soon

  • Goodbye, my friend!

    Adeus, meu amigo!

                            Breakdown:

                        Adeus: Goodbye
                        meu: my
                        amigo: friend

  • Bye, I'll see you tomorrow.

    Tchau, te vejo amanhã.

                            Breakdown:

                        Tchau: Bye
                        te: you (informal)
                        vejo: see
                        amanhã: tomorrow

  • Bye, have a nice day!

    Tchau, tenha um bom dia!

                            Breakdown:

                        Tchau: Bye
                        tenha: have
                        um: a (masculine singular)
                        bom: good
                        dia: day

  • Bye, take care!

    Tchau, cuide-se!

                            Breakdown:

                        Tchau: Bye
                        cuide-se: take care (reflexive imperative)

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

Job

emprego

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I have a job.

    Eu tenho um emprego.

                          Breakdown:

                      Eu: I
                      tenho: I have
                      um: a (masculine singular)
                      emprego: job

  • He is looking for a job.

    Ele está procurando emprego.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        está: is
                        procurando: looking for
                        emprego: job

  • My job is very demanding.

    Meu emprego é muito exigente.

                            Breakdown:

                        Meu: my
                        emprego: job
                        é: is
                        muito: very
                        exigente: demanding

  • I enjoy my job.

    Eu gosto do meu emprego.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I
                        gosto: I like
                        do: of the
                        meu: my
                        emprego: job

  • She lost her job.

    Ela perdeu o emprego.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ela: She
                        perdeu: lost
                        o: the
                        emprego: job

  • I want a job.

    Eu quero um emprego.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I
                        quero: I want
                        um: a (masculine singular)
                        emprego: job

  • I am looking for work.

    Procuro trabalho.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I - optonal
                        Procuro: I am looking for
                        trabalho: work

  • I need work.

    Preciso de trabalho.

                            Breakdown:

                        Preciso: I need
                        de: of
                        trabalho: work

  • I have experience.

    Tenho experiência.

                            Breakdown:

                        Tenho: I have
                        experiência: experience

  • Do you have a job

    Você tem emprego?

                            Breakdown:

                        Você: You (informal)
                        tem: have (present tense of "ter")
                        emprego: job

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

                          Breakdown:

                            Breakdown:

                        experiência: experience
                        em: in

                            Breakdown:

                        Esta: This is
                        é: is
                        a: the
                        minha: my
                        primeira: first
                        experiência: experience
                        de: of

                            Breakdown:

Shop

loja

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I like to go shopping.

    Gosto de ir às compras.

                        Breakdown:

                    Gosto: I like
                    de: of
                    ir: to go
                    às: to the (feminine plural)
                    compras: shopping

  • I went shopping yesterday.

    Fui às compras ontem.

                          Breakdown:

                      Fui: I went (past tense of "ir")
                      às: to the (feminine plural)
                      compras: shopping
                      ontem: yesterday

  • This shop sells clothes.

    Esta loja vende roupas.

                          Breakdown:

                      Esta: This
                      loja: shop
                      vende: sells
                      roupas: clothes

  • I need to go to the grocery shop.

    Preciso ir ao supermercado.

                          Breakdown:

                      Preciso: I need
                      ir: to go
                      ao: to the (masculine singular)
                      supermercado: supermarket

  • The shop is closed today.

    A loja está fechada hoje.

                          Breakdown:

                      A: The
                      loja: shop
                      está: is
                      fechada: closed
                      hoje: today

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.
