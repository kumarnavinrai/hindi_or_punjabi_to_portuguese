Hard

Duro

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • The exam was very hard.

    O exame foi muito difícil.

                          Breakdown:

                      O: The
                      exame: exam
                      foi: was
                      muito: very
                      difícil: hard

  • He works very hard.

    Ele trabalha muito.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        trabalha: works
                        muito: very
                        hard: hard (implied by "muito")

  • This rock is very hard.

    Esta pedra é muito dura.

                            Breakdown:

                        Esta: This
                        pedra: rock
                        é: is
                        muito: very
                        dura: hard

  • It's hard to believe he's gone.

    É difícil acreditar que ele se foi.

                            Breakdown:

                        É: It is
                        difícil: hard
                        acreditar: to believe
                        que: that
                        ele: he
                        se foi: has gone

  • I tried hard to pass the test.

    Eu tentei muito passar no teste.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I
                        tentei: I tried (past tense of "tentar")
                        muito: hard
                        passar: to pass
                        no: in the
                        teste: test

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !

                    ontem: yesterday
                    à: at the
                    noite: night

    Ela usa os mesmos sapatos todos os dias.

                          Breakdown:

                      Ela: She
                      usa: wears

                      mesmos: same
                      sapatos: shoes
                      todos: all
                      os: the
                      dias: days

  • We had the same problem last year.

    Tivemos o mesmo problema no ano passado.

                          Breakdown:

                      Tivemos: We had (past tense of "ter")
                      o: the
                      mesmo: same
                      problema: problem
                      no: in the
                      ano: year
                      passado: past

  • We have the same opinion.

    Temos a mesma opinião

                          Breakdown:

                      Temos: We have
                      a: the
                      mesma: same
                      opinião: opinion

  • They are twins, they look the same.

    Eles são gêmeos, parecem iguais.

                          Breakdown:

                      Eles: They

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.
