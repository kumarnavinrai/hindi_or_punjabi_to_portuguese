Lesson 15

Explore a variety of resources to master the beautiful language of European Portuguese!

Red

Vermelho

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • I touched my tongue.

    Toquei na língua.

                          Breakdown:

                      Toquei: I touched (past tense of "tocar")
                      na: on the (feminine)
                      língua: tongue

  • She stuck out her tongue.

    Ela mostrou a língua.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ela: She
                        mostrou: showed (past tense of "mostrar")
                        a língua: the tongue (feminine)

  • He burned his tongue on the hot coffee.

    Ele queimou a língua no café quente.

                            Breakdown:

                        Ele: He
                        queimou: burned (past tense of "queimar")
                        a língua: the tongue (feminine)
                        no: on the (masculine)
                        café quente: hot coffee

  • I can taste the food with my tongue.

    Posso saborear a comida com a língua.

                            Breakdown:

                        Posso: I can (present tense of "poder")
                        saborear: to taste
                        a comida: the food (feminine)
                        com: with
                        a língua: the tongue (feminine)

  • The cat licked its tongue.

    O gato lambeu a língua.

                            Breakdown:

                        O gato: The cat (masculine)
                        lambeu: licked (past tense of "lamber")
                        a língua: the tongue (feminine)

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !
