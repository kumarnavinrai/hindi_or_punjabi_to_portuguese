Red

Vermelho

Repeate every sentence 5 time loudly

  • The apple is red.

    A maçã é vermelha.

                          Breakdown:

                      A: The
                      maçã: apple
                      é: is
                      vermelha: red (feminine)

  • I have a red car.

    Eu tenho um carro vermelho.

                            Breakdown:

                        Eu: I
                        tenho: have
                        um: a (masculine singular)
                        carro: car
                        vermelho: red (masculine)

  • The traffic light is red.

    O semáforo está vermelho.

                            Breakdown:

                        O: The
                        semáforo: traffic light
                        está: is
                        vermelho: red (masculine)

  • I like to wear red clothes.

    Gosto de usar roupas vermelhas.

                            Breakdown:

                        Gosto: I like
                        de: to
                        usar: wear
                        roupas: clothes
                        vermelhas: red (feminine plural)

  • Red is my favorite color.

    Vermelho é a minha cor favorita.

                            Breakdown:

                        Vermelho: red
                        é: is
                        a: the
                        minha: my
                        cor: color
                        favorita: favorite

Now listen to these sentences and say them five times for practice. Try learning by writing the sentence down.

ਹੁਣ ਵਾਕ ਨੂੰ ਪੰਜ ਵਾਰ ਬੋਲੋ ਤੇ ਲਿਖ ਕੇ ਯਾਦ ਕਰਨ ਦੀ ਕੋਸ਼ਿਸ਼ ਕਰੋ !

अब वाकया को ५ वर बोले और लिख कर याद करने की कोशिश करें !
